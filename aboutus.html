Our Story

Welcome to Inventory Pro — your ultimate destination for high-quality PC parts and accessories. Established in March 2025 as an engineering mini project, our platform was born out of a passion for technology and the need for efficient inventory management.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize how individuals and businesses manage their PC hardware needs. Whether you're building your dream setup or upgrading a component, our mission is to make the process fast, easy, and affordable.

What We Offer

  • High-quality CPUs, GPUs, RAMs, and SSDs
  • Real-time inventory updates
  • Seamless booking in under 3 minutes
  • Custom PC builder tool
  • Delivery of your dream build in just 5 days

Why Choose Us?

We are more than a store — we are your tech partners. Our website offers detailed product info, reviews, and an easy-to-use interface.

Future Goals

Starting small, we aim to grow into full-service system assembly, nationwide delivery, and AI-based inventory prediction.
